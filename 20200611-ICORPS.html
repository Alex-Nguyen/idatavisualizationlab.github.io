Vung won the Spirit of NSF I-Corps Award

June 11, 2020

Vung Pham won the Spirit of NSF I-Corps Award.

The National Science Foundation's Innovation Corps (I-Corps) program uses experiential education to help researchers gain valuable insight into entrepreneurship, starting a business or industry requirements and challenges.

Vung won the Spirit of NSF I-Corps Award as the recognition for the team most exemplifying the spirit of I-Corps, the hard work, discipline, and intellectual honesty.
