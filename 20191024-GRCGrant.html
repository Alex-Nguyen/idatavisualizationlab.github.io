Huyen's proposal awarded from 2019 GRC/VSE Visionary Grants

August 4, 2019

Proposal of Visualizing Qualitative Data for Science and Education got funding for the 2019 GRC/VSE Visionary Grants

The Proposal of Visualizing Qualitative Data for Science and Education aims on exploring the potential in visualization of qualitative data and securing funding for larger programs and research, adapting approaches from data visualization to enhance qualitative study and address challenges in STEM education learning.

The Visionary Grants is a longstanding tradition of the Gordon Research Conference. The Visionary Grant program is supported by a grant from NASA and is intended to provide "seed funding" to enable collaborative teams of researchers the opportunity to develop a full research proposal for submission to appropriate grant agencies.

  • Huyen Nguyen, Ph.D. student, Department of Computer Science, Texas Tech University

in collaboration with:
