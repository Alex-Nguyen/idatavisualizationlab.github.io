Vung won the 3rd place at Herobots AI stock prediction

July 2, 2019

Congratulations Team IDVL_TTU for achieving 3rd Place in Biannual International AI Challenge!

Problem set: Create a price prediction module to predict the value of Bitcoin and Ethereum two hours ahead. Contestants were judged in accordance to the accuracy of their AI prediction models over the period of five months on cryptocurrency price prediction website 3PIKS.com.

The first entry submission deadline is on Wednesday, 23rd January 2019. These modules can be updated at any time after first submission until Monday, 20th May 2019. The prediction modules will be shown live on 3piks.com. The service is scheduled to launch on Tuesday, 15 January 2019.

There is no first place (due to the 70% accuracy criteria), so Zero cola is the 2nd place and TTU-IDVL is in the 3rd place.

IDVL_TTU team members:

  • Vung Pham, PhD student, Department of Computer Science, Texas Tech University.
  • Ngan Nguyen, PhD student, Department of Computer Science, Texas Tech University.
